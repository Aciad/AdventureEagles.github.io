The Gravity Factory

The gravity factory is a local indoor attraction and trampoline park here in Rexburg. The location has 'Air floors', 'Force beams', 'Gravity Dunk', an 'Inertia Board', 'Inverse Walls', a 'Kid Jump Floor', and many other attractions.

Location: 165 Airport Road, Rexburg ID 83440
Hours:
Monday through Thursday: 10am-9pm
Friday: 10am-12am
Saturday: 10am-11:30pm
Pricing: For individuals over 46 inches 13$ for the first hour, 3$ for socks and 7$ for each additional hour. For individuals under 46 inches 8$ for the first hour, 3$ for socks, and 5$ for each following hour.

The Legacy Flight Museum

The Legacy Flight Museum was founded in 2006 by John Bagley of Rexburg. The goal of the museum is to enable visitors to see military aircraft up close and personal and to remember the veterans who flew them. The museum collection includes aircraft from through the 20th century and occasionally performs airshows.

Location: 400 Airport Rd, Rexburg, ID 83440
Hours:
Winter hours (Labor Day to Memorial Day): Saturdays 10:00am to 4:00 pm
Summer hours (Memorial Day to Labor Day): Monday to Friday 9:00am to 5:00pm
Price: 6$ for every individual over 8 years of age. Family groups are 25$. Youth and Large Groups above 10 people are 3$ a person.
Contact Info:
Phone Number: 208 747 7615
