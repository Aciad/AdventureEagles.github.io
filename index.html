Outdoor Activities

Surrounded by stunning natural beauty, Rexburg is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Spend your summer days floating down the Teton River, exploring the massive Rexburg Sand Dunes, or hiking up nearby R-Mountain for an unforgettable sunset view. When winter rolls around, grab your snowboard or snowmobile and hit the world-class powder just a short drive away. Adventure is quite literally waiting right outside your back door.
